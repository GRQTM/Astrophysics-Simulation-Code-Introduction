## Quick snapshot

- **Method family**: Eulerian AMR (Adaptive Mesh Refinement)
- **Core traits**: Structured-grid AMR with extensible astrophysics modules
- **Typical usage**: AMR cosmological structure-formation and gas-dynamics workflows
- **Example used here**: `Hydro-1D/SodShockTube`
