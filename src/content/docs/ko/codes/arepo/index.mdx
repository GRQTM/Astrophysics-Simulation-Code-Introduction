## Quick snapshot

- **Method family**: Moving-mesh finite-volume hydrodynamics
- **Core traits**: Voronoi moving mesh + gravity + (with modules) MHD/RT
- **Typical usage**: Cosmological galaxy-formation runs with coupled gravity + hydrodynamics
- **Example used here**: `wave_1d`
