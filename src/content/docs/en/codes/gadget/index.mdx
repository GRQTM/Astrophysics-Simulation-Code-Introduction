## Quick snapshot

- **Method family**: TreePM gravity + particle hydrodynamics (SPH family)
- **Core traits**: Hierarchical gravity solver with particle-centric flow integration
- **Typical usage**: Cosmological N-body and galaxy-formation particle pipelines
- **Example used here**: official hydro sanity case
